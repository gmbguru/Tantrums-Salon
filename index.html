    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I book an appointment?

    You can book through WhatsApp, call us directly at +91 9220390222, or fill the booking form on our website. We offer flexible time slots for your convenience.

    What products do you use?

    We use premium salon-quality products from trusted brands. All products are dermatologically tested and safe for all hair and skin types.

    How long does bridal makeup take?

    Bridal makeup typically takes 60-90 minutes depending on complexity. We recommend a trial session before the actual event.

    Do you offer home service?

    Yes! We provide bridal makeup and special event makeup services at your home. Contact us for details and availability.

    What about cancellation policy?

    Cancellations made 24 hours in advance are free. For cancellations within 24 hours, a 50% charge applies.
